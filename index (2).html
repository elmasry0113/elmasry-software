*What is Software?

Software is a collection of programs, data, and instructions that enable a computer to perform specific tasks.

It is categorized into several main types:

*Application Software:

These are programs used by users to accomplish specific tasks, such as web browsers (Google Chrome, Firefox), word processors (Microsoft Word), and design applications (Adobe Photoshop).

*Operating Systems:

The fundamental software that manages computer resources and allows other programs to run, such as Windows, macOS, Linux, and Android.

*Middleware:

Software that acts as a bridge between operating systems and applications, facilitating their interaction.

*Open Source Software:

Programs that developers can modify and share freely, such as Linux and Apache.

*Artificial Intelligence & Cloud Computing:

At relies on machine learning and data analysis, such as AI assistants (ChatGPT, Siri) and cloud computing applications like Google Drive and Dropbox.
